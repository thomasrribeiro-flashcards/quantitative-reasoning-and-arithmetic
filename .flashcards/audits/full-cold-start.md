Deck: mathematics/quantitative-reasoning-and-arithmetic

Scope: all 10 approved chapters, 212 scheduled fronts, and 30 figures
Learner contract: no confirmed mathematical knowledge, subject knowledge, or
tools. Everyday words may be used; every mathematical term, symbol,
representation, unit, and procedure must be established on its own scheduled
front, in earlier scheduled feedback that later fronts can rely on, or in the

## Method and resolved closure

The chapter design and concept-dependency ledgers in CARD_README.md were
completed before large-scale authoring. For the audit, the ordered Q: and P:
fronts, including front figures and alt text, were considered without using
their paired answers to justify the same front. Answer-side terminology was
checked only after each front's dependency route was assigned. The approved
pilot's 18-row front record remains in pilot-cold-start.md; it was not rewritten
and no pilot card ID changed.

## Answer-side and mathematical verification

- Every scheduled block has one direct answer or solution, and every new block
  has a unique stable ID. The 18 approved pilot IDs are unchanged.
- Whole-number algorithms were checked by inverse operations and direct
  place-value decompositions.
- Division claims satisfy starting amount = divisor × quotient + remainder,
  with each remainder smaller than its divisor.
- Operation-order examples were recomputed from grouping, powers,
  equal-priority left-to-right rules, and addition/subtraction.
- Fraction and decimal conversions and operations were checked by
  simplification, inverse multiplication, common denominators, and bounds.
- Ratio tables were checked for constant unit rate. Percent changes were
  checked against the original baseline, including unequal successive changes.
- Unit conversions use the exact identities stated on their fronts. SI
  notation and decimal-prefix roles were checked against the 2026 BIPM SI
  Brochure, with NIST SP 811 retained as U.S. practical guidance. No source
  examples or figures were copied.

## Figures and inventory reconciliation

All 30 figures have editable TikZ source beside same-named generated SVGs and
load the shared root style. Each SVG has a responsive viewBox, generated
title/description accessibility metadata, high-contrast outlines, and a cue
beyond color. All were rasterized directly from the final SVG at a maximum
phone width of 360 pixels and inspected together and by changed subset. The
pattern-fill and conversion-map findings above were repaired and re-rendered.

The final inventory is 168 basic cards, 44 problem cards, zero clozes, and 30
figures. Chapter-level planned-versus-actual counts and the single explained
Chapter 9 form shift are recorded in CARD_README.md. No retrieval target or
planned figure role is missing.
